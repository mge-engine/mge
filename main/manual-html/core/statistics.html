Statistics¶

MGE provides a framework to gather and maintain statistics of operations and objects.

To use statistics, mge/core/statistics.hpp needs to be included.

Statistics Entry¶

Statistics entries group statistics values.

class statistics¶

A statistics entry.

A statistics entry covers a statistics topic, like the application, or a window, or a frame.

Subclassed by mge::root_statistics
